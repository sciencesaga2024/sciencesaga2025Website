Competition Instructions

General Science Quiz Rules

‚ñº
  • Teams must consist of maximum 2 members from the same institution
  • All participants must be higher secondary students
  • Questions will cover Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and General Science
  • Both written and oral rounds will be conducted
  • Use of mobile phones or any electronic devices is strictly prohibited
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding
  • Participants must bring their student ID cards

PowerPoint Presentation Guidelines

‚ñº
  • Choose any one topic from the given list
  • Presentation duration: 8-10 minutes + 2 minutes for Q&A
  • Maximum 15 slides (including title and reference slides)
  • Teams must bring their presentation in USB drive (backup recommended)
  • Original research and creative content will be given extra points
  • Plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification
  • Judging criteria: Content quality, presentation skills, creativity, and time management
